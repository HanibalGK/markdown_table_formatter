Markdown Table Formatter

This tool is a very early stage work in progress. When finished it will "Pretty Print" MultiMarkdown style tables.

Only the most basic, well-formed tables are currently processed correctly. Your Mileage May Vary.

To see what it's all about, try one of these examples, or format your own.
